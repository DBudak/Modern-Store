Modern Store App

Nobody has time to scroll through a boring grid of products anymore. In order to grab customer's attention you need to be interesting. And that's where Modern Store App comes in. Get your customers involved and guide them to a right product with a few questions about their needs and desires. This will help the customer to pick the right product right away and help store owners to get customers more interested and involved.
This is a demo of the Modern Store App. It uses shampoos as a simple example. Modern Store App works virtually for any product/service/store you can come up with.
Features
  • Gets your customers involved and engaged with your store
  • Works for any store/brand design
  • Works for any product/service
  • Perfectly scalable - works for any number of products/questions
  • Seamlessly integrates with your existing e-commerce store
